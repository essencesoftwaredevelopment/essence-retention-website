Data Deletion Instructions

Users can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data associated with the ESSENCE Notion Connector at any time.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, send an email to:

jacques@essencesoftwaredevelopment.com

What to Include

  • Your full name.
  • The email address connected to your account or integration.
  • The name of the Notion workspace or relevant Meta Ads account, if applicable.
  • A clear statement that you are requesting deletion of your data.

What Happens Next

After receiving a valid request, we will review it and handle the request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

We may contact you to verify your identity or clarify the scope of the deletion request before completing it.
